Inventory Audit - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Effective Date: March 23, 2025

Thank you for choosing Inventory Audit - Fast Barcode Scanner ("we," "our," or "app"). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or any other user data. All data created and managed within the Inventory Audit app remains solely on your device.

Camera Access

Our app requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes. We have built a custom user interface that utilizes Google's ML Kit for barcode recognition. The camera feed is never stored, transmitted, or processed outside of your device. All scanning operations happen locally on your device.

Storage Access

Our app may request storage permissions to allow you to export inventory reports to your device. We do not access any files outside of what you explicitly choose to save from our app.

Data Storage

All inventory data, including scanned barcodes, item descriptions, and any custom fields you create, are stored locally on your device. This data is not backed up to any cloud service by our app, nor is it transmitted to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google's ML Kit for barcode scanning functionality. This component operates entirely on your device and does not transmit scan data to Google or any other third party.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top of this page.
