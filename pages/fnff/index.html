Rules of Engagement

Bottom line? Do what it takes to survive.

Objective: 

Take charge of Team 1 and outwit the computer-controlled Team 2 in a relentless Friday Night FireFight.

Teams:

Two teams with three members each. Equipped with armor, health stims and ammo.

Actions:

  • Aim: Enhance your accuracy for the next shot, increasing your chances to hit.
  • Shoot: Fire at an opponent, reducing their health and armor.
  • Reload: Replenish your ammo to maintain firepower.
  • Take Cover: Seek refuge to minimize incoming damage, sacrificing attack potential.
  • Heal: Patch up injuries, restoring a portion of lost health.

Turn Sequence:

Team 1 selects one action for one team member.
Team 2's computer-controlled member automatically take action.

Victory Conditions:

Obliterate all of Team 2's members before Midnight when the sixth round concludes to make it through another Friday Night FireFight.

In this dystopian cyberpunk realm, strategic planning and synergy are your allies.
Monitor your team's health, armor, and ammo to make crucial tactical decisions.
Every move matters, as you navigate the perilous streets of this dark and unforgiving future.
Prepare for Friday Night FireFight, where it's man versus machine in a relentless battle for supremacy in the neon-tinged, tech-infested urban sprawl.
