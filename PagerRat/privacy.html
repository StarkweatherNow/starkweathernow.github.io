Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pager Rat ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. Pager Rat is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your location using GPS/Network providers for analytics.
  • We do not collect usage statistics or crash reports automatically.
  • We do not upload your files, scripts, or "loot" to any cloud server.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data strictly on your local device (in the Android App Sandbox):

  • SSH Keys & Credentials: Used to authenticate with your WiFi Pineapple. These are encrypted using the Android Keystore system.
  • Connection Logs: Saved locally for debugging purposes. You can clear these at any time in settings.
  • Downloaded Payloads: Scripts you explicitly download are stored in the app's private storage until you deploy them.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions for specific functionality:

  • INTERNET: Required to establish the SSH tunnel to your device.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to determine if you are connected to the correct WiFi network before attempting connection.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: (Optional) Required only if you wish to import payload files from your phone's Downloads folder.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@yourdomain.com
