HYBRID_ADVANTAGE

The Pager Rat ecosystem is built on a "Hybrid Distributed" philosophy. Rather than asking the Pager hardware to handle heavy user interfaces and database management tasks that drain the battery, we offload these operations to your Android device.

DATA_SOVEREIGNTY

We are dedicated to a single mission: keeping your data yours. We employ an in-depth defense strategy where your information is encrypted both during transmission (SSH/SSL) and at rest (AES-256). With an app-level passcode and hardware level encryption, extracting data from your device is mathematically improbable for unauthorized actors.
