The Invisible Wire

Security isn't a feature; it's the foundation. Pager Rat uses a custom SSH tunneling architecture to ensure your administration traffic never leaks.

  • Host Key Pinning: Prevents Man-in-the-Middle attacks by memorizing your device's fingerprint.
  • Ed25519 Keys: Modern elliptic curve cryptography for instant, secure authentication.
  • Hardware-Backed Storage: Keys are locked in the Android Keystore (TEE).
